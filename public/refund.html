Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Overview

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be issued for subscriptions to Rugby AI Predictor. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

2. Subscription Refund Eligibility

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you are not satisfied with the Service within 7 days of your initial purchase, you may request a full refund.

2.1 Eligible Refund Requests

Refunds may be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Within 7 Days of Purchase: Full refund available for any reason
  • Service Failure: If the Service is unavailable or non-functional for an extended period (more than 48 hours) due to technical issues on our end
  • Duplicate Charges: If you were charged multiple times for the same subscription
  • Unauthorized Purchase: If your payment method was used without your authorization

2.2 Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • After the 7-day money-back guarantee period has expired
  • If you have violated our Terms of Service
  • If you have shared or transferred your license key to another user
  • For partial subscription periods (e.g., mid-month cancellations)
  • If you simply changed your mind after the 7-day period
  • For subscription renewals (only initial purchases are eligible for the 7-day guarantee)

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact us using the email address associated with your subscription
  2. Include the following information in your request:
    • Your email address used for the subscription
    • Your license key (if available)
    • Date of purchase
    • Reason for refund request
    • Transaction ID or payment confirmation (if available)
  3. We will review your request and respond within 5-7 business days

Refund requests should be sent to the email address provided in your subscription confirmation email or through the contact method available in the Service.

4. Refund Processing

4.1 Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed

4.2 Refund Amount

For eligible refunds within the 7-day guarantee period, you will receive a full refund of the subscription fee paid. No partial refunds will be issued for unused portions of the subscription period.

4.3 License Key Deactivation

Upon approval of a refund, your license key will be immediately deactivated, and you will lose access to premium features. The license key cannot be reactivated after a refund has been processed.

5. Subscription Cancellation

Important: Canceling your subscription is different from requesting a refund:

  • Cancellation: Stops future renewals but does not provide a refund for the current billing period
  • Refund: Returns your payment and deactivates your license key

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting us. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period. No refunds will be provided for the remaining subscription period unless you are within the 7-day guarantee window.

6. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you initiate a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider instead of contacting us directly, we reserve the right to:

  • Immediately deactivate your license key
  • Permanently ban your account from future subscriptions
  • Challenge the chargeback with evidence of service delivery

We strongly encourage you to contact us directly first to resolve any issues before initiating a chargeback. We are committed to resolving customer concerns fairly and promptly.

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Technical Issues

If you experience persistent technical issues that prevent you from using the Service, please contact our support team. We will work with you to resolve the issue. If the problem cannot be resolved and significantly impacts your ability to use the Service, we may consider a refund on a case-by-case basis, even outside the 7-day guarantee period.

7.2 Service Discontinuation

In the unlikely event that we discontinue the Service, we will provide prorated refunds for the unused portion of your subscription period.

8. Currency and Payment Methods

Refunds will be issued in the same currency and to the same payment method used for the original purchase. If the original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to arrange an alternative refund method.

9. Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on this page. For existing subscribers, the refund policy in effect at the time of purchase will apply to their subscription.

10. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need to request a refund, please contact us through the Service or via the contact information provided in your subscription confirmation email.

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 5-7 business days and process approved refunds within 5-10 business days.
